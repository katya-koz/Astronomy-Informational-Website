Astronomy

Astronomy a natural science which explores celestial bodies and other phenomena. It uses Chemistry, Mathematics and Physics to explain the origins of our universe. Ancient Greek Philosophers were the first to notice the celestial bodies in our universe, calling them "planets", which means wanderers in Greek.

The science is influenced by leaders like Charles Messier, Annie Jump Cannon, Eratosthenes of Cyrene, Nicholas Copernicus, and many more. Astronomy is actually one of the only sciences which utilizes amateurs in the field.

The influence of Astronomy has sprung up many technologies which we use today, such as satellites, spaceships, and telescopes.

Being one of the oldest fields of studies, Astronomy has a rich history, all the way from ancient to modern times.

Despite Astronomy being a vast field, there are plenty of mysteries yet to be explained... Dark matter, the size of the universe, dark energy... Are there really answers to life's greatest mysteries?
